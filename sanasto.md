**Wa-Tor**

The Hunt level. Dewdney, 1984. A toroidal predator-prey simulation — sharks and fish,
populations cycling on a world with no edge. From station time, this is what a
civilization looks like: simple rules, expendable individuals, patterns that rise
and collapse between shifts.

Only the Game of Life level in Kaamos will be Wa-Tor.

**Tapahtuu. Kaksi kertaa.**

It happens. Twice.

The acknowledgment that requires no explanation. Sent when the thing
has already happened and there is nothing more to say about it.
