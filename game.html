about

Kaamos is simultaneously a novel, a video game, a music project, and a cosmological framework. They are not separate projects that share a name. They are the same project in four registers.

The novel follows Eerikki (Edgar), who caused a cosmological disruption and is genuinely trying to fix it while making it worse for everyone except himself. The game teaches you the novel's language and cosmology without announcing that it's doing so.

The universe consists of branes separated by the void. The void has no spacetime but it has structure. The structure is navigable. The navigation is the game. Hiljaisuus — the Silence, Station 9 — sits at the center of the Expanse, a fold in the brane topology that is always present.

Dual Correctness

Two truths collide. Both are valid. Neither resolves. No synthesis. The collision is the meaning. This is not a theme. It is the load-bearing structure.

This is the skeleton of that universe in playable form. It teaches by doing. No explanation. Just the Silence, drawing something. You color it in.
